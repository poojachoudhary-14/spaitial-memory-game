How to Play

Rule mechanics & progression mapping

1

Memorize Targets: At the start of each level, a few blocks will appear and highlight in Gold. Focus and lock their locations in your mind!

2

Grid Populates: After 2.5 seconds, the rest of the board fills up with new block distractors. All cells then hide their faces.

3

Recall & Match: Click ONLY the initial blocks you memorized. A correct choice reveals a green check. An error costs 1 Life ❤️ and reveals the solution briefly to allow a second attempt!

4

Dynamic Evolution: As you conquer levels, grid boundaries expand (from 3x3 up to 6x6) and block sequences get longer, pushing your working memory limits!

A cognitive science experiment. Designed for spatial working memory enhancement.
